Hi there! I am João.

I am a graphic designer, content creator, videographer and marketeer. I was born with a passion for illustration and drawing, which evolved to a passion for digital content. I also have a deep interest in Virtual Reality.

Currently based in Utrecht and working at 5CA in Utrecht as a Visual content creator. Previously worked at Pakhuis de Zwijger in Amsterdam and also in the Museum of Art, Architecture and Technology (MAAT) in Lisbon, did some freelancer content creation and worked as an art assistant for a reality show.

Bachelor degree in Multimedia Arts at the University of Fine Arts in Lisbon.

Programming & 3D Design

The VR Gallery

Project Link

In this project, I brought together various previous experiences and projects from my art and multimedia studies, combining curation techniques, virtual modeling, and model-making to create a virtual and physical exhibition. Programming & 3D Design

Pravda

Project Link

For the Systems course, I created “PRAVDA”, an interactive installation that utilizes a playful system of conflict interaction between truth and lies to engage the participants. Using Twitter feeds as a starting point, their mission is to identify the truth and expose the lies in the media, working together to combat disinformation. In order to effectively do so, the participants must develop critical thinking skills and strategize their actions, ultimately striving for a ‘liberating truth’. To bring this project to life, I utilized a range of tools including Blender, Unity, and Virtual Reality software.

What I Do

Here are some of
the key areas
I specialize in

Product Design

I create digital products that balance functionality with aesthetic appeal, always placing the user at the center of the process. My approach combines design thinking, prototyping and iteration to ensure usability and accessibility. I work mainly with Figma and Adobe XD to deliver intuitive and engaging product experiences.

Branding

I develop strong and consistent brand identities that communicate the values and personality of a business. From logo design to color systems and typography, I build visual languages that make brands stand out. Using tools like Illustrator, InDesign and Photoshop, I craft assets that work across print and digital.

Video Editing

I produce video content that tells stories in a dynamic and professional way, from event highlights to promotional campaigns. My process includes editing, sound design and motion graphics to create an impactful final product. I work with Premiere Pro, After Effects and Audacity to ensure polished and creative results.

UX Research

I conduct user research to uncover insights that improve digital products, focusing on usability testing, interviews and user flows. These findings inform design decisions and ensure solutions meet real needs. With Figma, Notion and prototyping tools, I structure and document processes that support better user experiences.

Illustration

I create original illustrations that bring projects to life, whether for branding, editorial or digital interfaces. My style adapts to each project, from expressive drawings to clean vector artwork. Using Photoshop, Illustrator and Procreate, I develop visuals that add character and clarity to communication.

Photography

I capture stories through photography, documenting events, creating portraits and exploring urban and creative projects. My goal is to combine technical quality with atmosphere and authenticity in each image. I shoot with DSLR and mirrorless cameras, and edit in Lightroom and Photoshop to achieve professional results.
